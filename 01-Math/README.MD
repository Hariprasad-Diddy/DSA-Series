## How to Approach Math Problems

- Start with a **brute-force idea**
- Optimize using **mathematical observations**
- Look for:
  - Divisibility rules
  - Logarithmic reductions
  - Symmetry and patterns

---

## Interview Notes

- Math problems often test **logic**, not syntax
- Always consider **edge cases**:
  - `0`
  - Negative numbers
  - Large inputs
- Explain *why* a formula works, not just the result
